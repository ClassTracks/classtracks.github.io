Why?

We want you to be able to take advantage of all the benefits of ClassTracks before you buy.

Customization

You know your students best. ClassTracks is fully customizable so you can create the best lessons for your students. Upload your own vocabulary and images, and activate audio to create an experience unique to your classroom and teaching style.

Scientifically Proven

ClassTracks uses spaced-repetition and scaffolding to help build and retain students’ memory of target language vocabulary. Words that were more challenging for students to learn are shown more often.

Data Insights

You have access to your class’s data so that you can see where students are struggling. Students can see areas where they can improve by looking at easy-to-read graphs and charts.
