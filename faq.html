Who can use ClassTracks?

ClassTracks is a classroom resource for language teachers. When a school or teacher signs up for ClassTracks, students associated with that account can make their own logins. Students and parents cannot use ClassTracks unless their school or teacher opens an account.

How much does ClassTracks cost?

We’re committed to keeping ClassTracks affordable and accessible. Contact us today for your price quote.

Can I do a trial period?

Yes! Teachers can try ClassTracks for free for 30 days.

Can I purchase ClassTracks for myself or my child?

No, currently we only provide service for teachers and schools. Sign up for our newsletter to stay up to date on any new offerings from ClassTracks.

What communicative activities can teachers do to help students better communicate using the vocabulary they are learning on ClassTracks?

There are lots of lessons, activities, and suggestions about this topic on our blog, we’d love for you to check it out!

Is ClassTracks safe for my child?

Yes. We are committed to keeping information about everyone who uses our product private, and use the latest and most recommended in security technology to keep your data protected:

  • OAuth2, a standard authentication protocol to connect to external services like Google Classroom and Edmodo
  • bcrypt, a standard encryption library for storing passwords and other data
  • Secure https connections
  • Encrypted Mongo database
