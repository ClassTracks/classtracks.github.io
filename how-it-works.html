Customize

Customize your vocabulary list modules in any language and writing system and assign them into units for your class.

Audio and Visual Add-ons

ClassTracks allows for multiple acceptable answers and makes it easy for teachers to associate each term with both visual and auditory cues.

Gamified Interface

Our product is centered around a gamified interface where students are challenged to actively recall each term before seeing the correct answer. ClassTracks uses spaced repetition so students repeat words exactly when they need to see them for optimal retention.

Teacher Feedback

Teachers have instant access to an easy-to read dashboard showing student data. The data can depict a whole class, or can be customized to show an individual student for a more detailed report.
