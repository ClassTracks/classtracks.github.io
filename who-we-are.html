Our Mission

At ClassTracks, we empower our language teachers by making the process of teaching a new language simple. Founded in 2014, our company is made for teachers, by teachers, to transition language education to the 21st century classroom. Our platform offers immediate feedback for both students and teachers during and after each session, research-backed design featuring a gamified interface, and fully customizable content. So what are you waiting for? Join us and many more teachers nationwide today.
